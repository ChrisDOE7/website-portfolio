Who am I:

Welcome to my website! I'm Chris and I'm just learning how to create stunning web content. A new and exciting topic that I would like to explore as soon as possible is the world of automations. In short "Bots". Everybody who is often and gladly on the web has his certain procedures, which are already subconsciously stored in a form of automation. Every interaction with the computer that requires a manual reaction is (for the computer) wasted time, because the human brain first has to absorb the received information, then process it and finally react to it. Let's turn our disadvantage into an advantage and let the machine do some of the tedious work itself, so that we ourselves have more time for the important things.
